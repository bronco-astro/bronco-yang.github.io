About

I am a PhD student in Physics at Rice University with research interests in exoplanet detection and characterization, protoplanetary disks, and planet formation. My work combines observational data analysis with numerical modeling and statistical methods.

I have experience working with data from space-based observatories, including JWST and eROSITA, as well as developing and analyzing simulations of disk dynamics. I am particularly interested in connecting observations with physical models to understand planet formation processes.
